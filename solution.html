Igarapé Institute

The Igarapé is a stitucions that fight for and support safety, security and development at Global South based on Brazil. They mobilizes to analyse data about Building peace, Drug Policy, Citizen Security, Safer Cities and Cyber Security to understand better the world’s future. The institute distributes publics many articles, policy reports, newspapers and open source application that analyze situations in actual highlight on the South, and in some level all the world. There are some of the supported projects articles, about Homicide of children and teenagers int Brazil, Central African Republic: historical roots and immediate causes of conflict until The Smart Policing, financed also by the UK Department for International Development.

Serrapilheira Institution

Officially launched in March 2017. Serrapilheira is a private institution, without profit, that selects and finance proposals of research projects and outreaches chemistry, computer science, earth sciences, engineering, life sciences, mathematics and physics. The institution appreciate decrease the visibility and impact of science in Brazil, trying to bring opportunity, freedom and resources to their proposers.The selection of Proposals happens through call for submissions, were the interested send their proposal. The Scientific advisory Board and Executive Team select the proposal following the criterias The proposer must have obtained his or her doctorate after 2007, have affiliations with Brazilian institutions and their years of experience with research. The funds come from the institute - unlike other financing sources, but they reflect to incorporate it in the future, “as the institute matures” -, with an initial incoming of R$350 million, with a amount of R$15 million resources available.

Entropia Coletiva

Created by Patrícia Bado and Frederico Reis in 2016. At the begin, the platform only attempted the biological, chemical and mathematical departament. But the success of it led to expand their horizons to several types of researches. Like about theories about combating Alzheimer obesity and the science inside the Quilombos communities(quilombolas). The operation of the plataform depend of the he contributor and the requirers proposes the amounts they agree to contribute. The Entropia focus their work in small projects beside they don’t intend substitute Estate investment, the platform must be for emergencial moments or to small projects without condition to justify the own funding to the government.Experiências tropicais de angústia(tropical experiences of anguish) , from the historian Natália Fontes Rodrigues(UFRJ) is a successful project financed by the platform.(arrecadou R$ 9.855).
